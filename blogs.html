Travel Smarter with Delight Tips

Quick, actionable wisdom from our experienced travel team — because great trips start with great planning.

🛂

Visa Planning

Apply 4–6 weeks early for peak-season destinations. Keep all documents digital and physical.

💸

Best Time to Book

Book 6–8 weeks ahead for best fares. Tuesdays and Wednesdays consistently show the lowest prices.

🌦️

Check the Season

SE Asia has monsoon season — avoid Bali in Dec–Feb. Maldives is best October to April.

📦

Pack Light

A 7kg carry-on is enough for two weeks. Roll clothes, use packing cubes, skip "just in case" items.

💳

Forex Card

Get a loaded forex card before you travel. No foreign transaction fees and better exchange rates.

📡

Stay Connected

Buy a local SIM at the airport or use Airalo eSIM. Activate before you land for seamless connectivity.

🛡️

Travel Insurance

Always insure your trip. Medical emergencies abroad without insurance can cost ₹10 lakh or more.

📸

Golden Hour Shots

Shoot between 7–9 AM and 5–7 PM for the best travel photos. Your phone camera is enough.
