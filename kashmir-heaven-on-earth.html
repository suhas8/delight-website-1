Itinerary (Day Wise)

Day 01 : Arrival in Srinagar

  • Arrival at Srinagar Airport and warm welcome by our local representative.
  • Transfer to hotel / houseboat and complete check-in process.
  • Evening shikara ride on Dal Lake with scenic sunset views.
  • Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (as per itinerary plan).

Vegetarian and non-vegetarian food options available.

Day 02 : Srinagar Local Sightseeing

  • Mughal Garden circuit covering Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.
  • Old Srinagar exploration with local market walk and handicraft shops.
  • Photo stops at scenic viewpoints around Dal Lake boulevard.
  • Evening leisure with optional local food tasting.

Breakfast and Dinner included.

Try authentic Kashmiri Kahwa in local cafes.

Day 03 : Gulmarg Excursion

  • Drive to Gulmarg through pine valleys and mountain roads.
  • Gondola experience (ticket subject to availability/weather).
  • Leisure activities such as snow play or meadow walk depending on season.
  • Return to Srinagar for relaxed evening and dinner.

Breakfast and Dinner included.

Carry gloves and warm layers for upper-gondola zones.

Day 04 : Sonmarg Day Tour

  • Journey to Sonmarg along the Sindh river with glacier viewpoints.
  • Optional pony ride to nearby scenic points (direct payment basis).
  • Photography and leisure time in meadow landscapes.
  • Return drive to Srinagar by evening.

Breakfast and Dinner included.

Best day for panoramic mountain photos.

Day 05 : Pahalgam Excursion

  • Scenic drive to Pahalgam through saffron and apple belt landscapes.
  • Riverfront leisure and optional local activities in valley points.
  • Explore local market for dry fruits and Kashmiri souvenirs.
  • Return to Srinagar for final evening stay.

Breakfast and Dinner included.

Great day for local handicraft shopping.

Day 06 : Departure

  • Morning breakfast and smooth hotel checkout.
  • Airport transfer with driver assistance.
  • Trip closure support and future travel recommendations.
  • Departure with unforgettable Kashmir memories.

Breakfast included.

Airport drop as per your departure timing.
