🧭 Overview

Bali Complete Adventure offers comprehensive cultural and adventure experiences across 6N/7D. Start with iconic Ubud & Kintamani highlights including Tegenungan waterfall, Tegalalang terraces, coffee tasting, Alas Harum swing. Discover dramatic West Nusa Penida island, explore North Bali's Ulun Danu Temple, Handara Gate, Banjar Hot Springs. Thrill with ATV quad bike trails, water sports (banana boat, jet ski, parasailing), and Tanah Lot sunset temple. Perfect Bali immersion!
