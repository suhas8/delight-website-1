Itinerary (Day Wise)

Day 01 : Bangkok - Pattaya Transfer + Pattaya Exploration

  • Breakfast at Tiger Topia to start the day with an exciting themed experience.
  • Visit Big Buddha Hill for panoramic views and spiritual charm.
  • Enjoy Alcazar Show with dazzling performances in Pattaya.
  • Explore Street Food Market for local flavours and city vibes.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

All Indian meals are included.

Day 02 : Pattaya Exploration

  • Coral Island Tour with speedboat ride and beach time.
  • Visit Floating Market for shopping and local culture.
  • Walking Street Exploration in the evening for Pattaya nightlife atmosphere.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Keep beachwear and spare clothes for island activities.

Day 03 : Pattaya Exploration

  • Gems Gallery visit to witness gem artistry and shopping options.
  • Nong Nooch Garden tour with landscaped gardens and cultural vibes.
  • Traditional Thai Massage for relaxing wellness experience. (optional)
  • Pattaya Beach Road Exploration at leisure.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Free time available for shopping at select stops.

Day 04 : Pattaya - Bangkok + Bangkok Exploration

  • Pattaya to Bangkok transfer in comfortable AC transportation.
  • Bangkok Temple Tour covering key city landmarks.
  • Visit shopping area for souvenirs and local products.
  • Dinner Cruise with city skyline views on the river.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Smart casual attire recommended for dinner cruise.

Day 05 : Checkout - Safari World - Departure

  • Checkout from hotel and proceed for sightseeing.
  • Safari World visit for wildlife encounters and themed attractions.
  • Departure transfer as per flight schedule.

Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Departure assistance included for smooth check-out and transfer.
